Current Friction force expalined : When F is applied and is less then Maximum frictional force, contact between lower and upper block will resist the movement by applying force equal to F in opposite direction.
Confusion regarding frictional force being maximum frictional force can be eliniminated by this logic: If frictional force is maximum frictional force and applied force is less then it, then upper block should move backword.
